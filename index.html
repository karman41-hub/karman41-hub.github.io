About

Metallurgist by passion, materials scientist by education, and a researcher by curiosity — I forge innovative solutions in continuous casting through physical modelling, unlock value from waste streams through metal recovery and bio-based agglomeration, and bridge the gap between fundamental metallurgical science and industrial application.

Based in Sweden, I work within the research ecosystem at Swerim AB and the Nordic steel industry, collaborating with industrial partners on challenges spanning continuous casting process optimisation, sustainable ferrochrome production, and next-generation green steelmaking routes.

My research has produced 13 publications and conference contributions with over 163 citations, covering areas from alloy characterisation and high-temperature metallurgy to circular metallurgy and bio-based binder systems for iron and steel production.

I am actively seeking opportunities in advanced industrial research, PhD programmes in process metallurgy or computational metallurgy, and consulting engagements where deep technical expertise creates measurable value.
